🔒 Privasi & ringan

• Tanpa iklan, tanpa SDK analitik, tanpa pelacakan
• 100% offline — tidak ada koneksi internet sama sekali
• Ukuran mungil, ringan di perangkat

Dibuat dengan prinsip "lakukan satu hal, lakukan dengan benar". Tanpa fitur basi, tanpa gangguan — hanya cahaya saat Anda membutuhkannya.

Catatan: fitur LED memerlukan perangkat yang memiliki lampu flash. Pada perangkat tanpa flash, gunakan mode Senter Layar.

### Full description
```
A simple flashlight that does one thing perfectly: turning on your phone's LED as fast as possible — no clutter, no ads, and no permission requests at all.

⚡ Why it's different
Most flashlight apps ask for permissions that make no sense (camera, location, contacts) and then show ads. Senter doesn't. It controls the LED flash directly through the Android system, so it needs NO camera permission and no permissions whatsoever. Your privacy stays intact.

🔦 Features
• Instant on/off with one big button
• Automatic SOS — emergency Morse pattern (· · · — — — · · ·)
• Screen Light mode — a full bright white screen when you need light without the LED
• Quick Settings Tile — toggle straight from the quick panel
• Light & dark mode
• Auto-off when the app is closed, to save battery

🔒 Privacy & lightweight
• ZERO system permissions — no camera, location, storage, or anything else
• No ads, no analytics SDKs, no tracking
• 100% offline — no internet connection at all
• Tiny size, light on your device

Built on the principle of "do one thing, do it right." No stale features, no distractions — just light when you need it.

Note: the LED feature requires a device that has a flash. On devices without one, use Screen Light mode.
```

## ⚙️ Catatan ASO (anti-penolakan)
- **Tidak ada keyword-stuffing** — kata "senter/flashlight/LED" muncul wajar, tidak diulang berlebihan.
- **Tidak ada klaim menyesatkan** — semua fitur yang disebut benar-benar ada di app.
- **Tidak ada bahasa promosi terlarang** (mis. "#1", "terbaik", "gratis selamanya") yang memicu filter.
- Judul memuat deskriptor jujur ("Lampu LED Nol Izin") — membantu discovery tanpa melanggar.
- Klaim "nol izin / no data" **didukung bukti**: manifest nol `<uses-permission>` + forensik APK.
